Our Mission

WasteWise was created as part of the DigiPen 2025 Spring Hackabyte Hackathon with the goal of reducing waste in school cafeterias. By leveraging past user data, WasteWise predicts order volumes to help schools optimize their food preparation and minimize waste.

Why It Matters

Food waste is a significant issue in schools, leading to unnecessary costs and environmental impact. WasteWise aims to tackle this problem by providing actionable insights that empower schools to make data-driven decisions.

How It Works

Our platform analyzes historical data on food orders and consumption patterns. Using predictive algorithms, WasteWise forecasts future demand, enabling schools to prepare the right amount of food and reduce excess waste.
